# RefreshView Browser
_Your Internet Refreshed_

Remember when the web was simple? We bring you faster loading without Big Tech or AI tracking.
Browse with built-in ad blockers, safer social apps, private search & better security.

## Connecting Social Communities
We designed a gorgeous dashboard that seamlessly connects you with a global ecosystem, integrating with apps like [PrivacySafe Social](https://privacysafe.social). Join a thriving community of over 1,000 servers and millions of users on the decentralized Mastodon network. RefreshView Browser is still in alpha testing, but you can [Sign Up for PrivacySafe Social Now](https://privacysafe.social).

## Power Under the Hood
RefreshView bundles our suite of [PrivacySafe apps](https://privacysafe.app) with features like tree-style tabs, workspace switching & sidebar bookmarks.

## Worry-Free Search Engine
RefreshView Search indexes 82 sources and delivers results with no ads or user profiling.

## Privacy By Default
Protect your browsing with add-ons by the Electronic Frontier Foundation & find hidden smartphone trackers with Exodus Privacy.

## Technical Information
- Based on Firefox & [Pulse Browser](https://github.com/pulse-browser)
- Built using [refreshview-glue](https://github.com/PrivacySafe/refreshview-glue) (gluon)
- Includes [uBlock Origin](https://github.com/gorhill/uBlock)
- Includes [Privacy Badger](https://github.com/EFForg/privacybadger)
- Includes[Exodify](https://github.com/FacettsOpen/exodify)
- Includes[SearXNG](https://github.com/searxng/searxng)
- Vertical tabs made available by the team at [Fushra Pulse](https://github.com/pulse-browser)
